Our Story

Lumixsa AI was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to democratize artificial intelligence and make advanced tools accessible to everyone, regardless of technical background or location.

Founded and managed by Dilshad Ahmad, a seasoned developer with over 10 years of experience in software development and AI integration, Lumixsa AI represents the culmination of a vision to bridge the gap between complex technology and everyday users.

We believe that AI should not be confined to tech giants or research institutions. Everyone deserves access to tools that can enhance their productivity, creativity, and decision-making capabilities.
